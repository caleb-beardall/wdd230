Membership Levels

Gold Membership

  • VIP access to all Chamber events
  • Included in all Chamber marketing content
  • Permitted a seat on the Chamber Advisory Council
  • One-on-one consultation with Chamber leadership

Silver Membership

  • Invited to all periodic Chamber events
  • Included in the majority of Chamber marketing content
  • Permitted to vote on the Chamber Advisory Council
  • Exclusive benefits package to meet business goals

Bronze Membership

  • Invited to all quarterly Chamber events
  • Included in selective Chamber marketing content
  • Permitted to participate on the Chamber Advisory Council
  • Premium benefits package to meet business goals

NP Membership

  • Invited to annual Chamber event
  • Included in selective Chamber marketing content
  • Receives Chamber Advisory Council communications
  • Standard benefits package to meet business goals
